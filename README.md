# 🌾 KrishiRaksha

## AI-Powered Agricultural Crisis Response Platform

KrishiRaksha is an intelligent crisis response platform designed to help farmers and local traders respond proactively to agricultural risks caused by **market volatility, climate uncertainty, and post-harvest losses**.

Built under the **Rapid Crisis Response** theme for **Google Solution Challenge 2026**, KrishiRaksha leverages AI, predictive analytics, and real-time alerts to help farmers **predict risks, prevent losses, and respond intelligently.**

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Smallholder farmers often suffer significant losses due to:

- Sudden fluctuations in crop market prices  
- Unpredictable rainfall and climate conditions  
- Lack of timely sowing/harvesting guidance  
- Post-harvest spoilage and inadequate storage access  
- Limited access to real-time decision support

These recurring crises impact farmer livelihoods, food security, and rural resilience.

---

## 💡 Solution

KrishiRaksha provides a unified agricultural crisis intelligence platform through:

### 📈 Market Crisis Alerts
- Real-time crop price monitoring  
- Price volatility detection  
- Sell/Hold recommendations  
- Farmer-trader marketplace linkage

### 🌦 Climate & Sowing Advisory
- Weather-informed crop planning  
- Rainfall alerts  
- Sowing and harvesting recommendations

### 🌾 Post-Harvest Protection
- Spoilage risk detection  
- Storage guidance  
- Waste reduction support

### 🤖 AI Farmer Copilot
- Multilingual AI assistant  
- Personalized recommendations  
- Voice-enabled farmer support
